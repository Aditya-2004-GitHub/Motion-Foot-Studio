About Pre-Production

Pre-production is the most critical phase of any animation or video project. At MF Studios, we focus on building a strong creative and technical foundation before execution.

This stage ensures clarity, reduces errors, and streamlines the entire production pipeline — saving time, cost and ensuring everyone is aligned before a single frame is produced.

Strong creative planning
